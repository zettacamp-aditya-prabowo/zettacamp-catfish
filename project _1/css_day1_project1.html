Super Hero Profile

Superman


Debut hero in June 1938 and published April 18, 1938.

Superpower

  • Superhuman strength, speed, stamina and durability
  • Enhanced vision
    1. Heat vision
    2. Telescopic vision
    3. X-ray vision
  • Freeze and wind breath
  • Flight
